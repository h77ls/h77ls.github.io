Conway's Game of Life

Conways Game of Life is a cellular automaton create by mathematician John H. Conway in 1970. It takes place on a square grid, with each square having 2 states; dead or alive. The state of each square is determined by the states of it's neighbor squares. Many simple patterns can become very complex.

Survivalcraft 2

Survivalcraft is a game all about surviving. You must learn to survive in an infinite world filled with danger. You need a fire before the cold night comes, food before starvation sets in, and shelter before the wild animals find you. Take a wrong step and you will fall into a pit trap. Everything can kill you.


Galimulator

Galimulator is a game about galactic empires. Watch hundreds of empires fight for control of the galaxy, or take control of one yourself. Enter sandbox mode and play god, helping your choosen nation on the path of victory, or rid the galaxy of all life.


Diplomacy

Diplomacy was originally a board game created in 1954 by Allan B. Calhamer, but now has many online varients. This game is all about strategy, trust, and lies. Form alliances with other nations and split Europe between them, or stab them in the back and take it all for yourself.
